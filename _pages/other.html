Science outreach

I have been involved in public engagement and science outreach since my undergraduate studies. I have given numerous talks at events taking place in Glasgow Science Centre, the Francis Crick Institute as well as in the Department of Biochemistry at the University of Oxford, where I was a part of the outreach team.

Classical singing

I have been an avid singer since I was 7 years old. I performed numerous times at the University of Oxford, including in March 2022 to raise money in support of Ukraine.

Rowing
